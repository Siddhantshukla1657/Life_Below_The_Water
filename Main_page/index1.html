About Marine Life

Marine life refers to all living organisms that inhabit the oceans, from the tiniest plankton to the largest whales. Oceans cover over 70% of the Earth’s surface, making marine life vital for the planet’s ecological balance.

Discover Marine Life

Marine Conservation

Marine life faces numerous threats, including pollution, overfishing, climate change, and habitat destruction. It is crucial to protect marine ecosystems to preserve the biodiversity of our oceans. Join the effort in conserving marine life.
